Getting Started

Registration

How do I register my player?

Use the Register Now button on the homepage or this page. It opens the official 2026 NWCC Fall Kickball registration page in LeagueApps.

What is the registration fee?

The registration fee is $50 per player.

Can my child register without already having a team?

Yes. Players may register individually. NWCC will work to place them based on eligibility, available coaches, and open roster space.

Is team placement guaranteed?

No. Placement depends on registration numbers, coach availability, age eligibility, and available roster space.

02

Players & Teams

Eligibility & Placement

What age groups can participate?

NWCC plans to offer Rookies through Teenage divisions. Final eligibility follows the official league age chart.

Can my player select any team?

Players should only join a team approved by NWCC. Players needing placement should register individually.

How will I know which team my child is assigned to?

NWCC will contact the parent or guardian after coaches, rosters, and assignments are finalized.

Can friends or siblings request the same team?

Families may submit a request, but placement cannot be guaranteed because roster balance and available space must be considered.

03

Season Information

Games & Scheduling

When does the season begin and end?

The planned season dates are September 21 through November 13.

What days will games be played?

Games are planned for Mondays and Thursdays.

When will the complete schedule be posted?

The schedule will be posted after registration closes, coaches are confirmed, and rosters are finalized.

What happens when weather affects a game?

Cancellations and makeup information will be shared through official league communication channels and Facebook.

04

Staying Informed

Communication & Policies

How will parents receive league updates?

Updates may be provided through LeagueApps, email, and the official NWCC Fall Kickball Facebook page.

Where can I review the league rules and bylaws?

Use the Rules & Bylaws page in the navigation menu.

Who should I contact if registration information is incorrect?

Use the Contact page and include the player’s name and a clear explanation of what needs to be corrected.
