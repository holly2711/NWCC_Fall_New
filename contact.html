Why We Volunteer

For the Players and Families

NWCC board members volunteer because youth sports can build confidence, friendships, responsibility, and lasting memories. Their goal is to create a league where families feel informed, welcomed, and supported.

Our Commitment

Positive League Leadership

The board is committed to fair league operations, clear communication, responsible decision-making, good sportsmanship, and a fun experience for all players.
