Our Story

NWCC Fall Kickball League continues the tradition of NWCC Little Miss Kickball by creating an organized fall program for returning and new players. The league is designed to keep children active, strengthen skills, and provide families with another opportunity to enjoy the sport together.

We welcome players across the Rookies, Peewees, Juniors, Seniors, and Teenage age groups (ages 3-18).
